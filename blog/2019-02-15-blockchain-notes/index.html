Bitcoin scalability issues

Number of transactions network can confirm in the certain amount of time.

  • Bitcoin blocks are created every 10 min, and a block can hold 1MB of transaction March of 2018. It means bitcoin blockchain can process 3 transactions a second.
  • In 2015, transaction volume exceeded network's capacity so much, blocks run out of space meaning that transactions are not included in blockchain and unconfirmed. It led to proposals such as Bitcoin XT, Bitcoin Classic, Segregated witness (SegWit)

Bitcoin mechanics & Optimizations

  • bitcoin address includes 3 things: public key, version byte(which network we're on), checksum(to avoid human error)
